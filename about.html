Sumeru Akademiya or GenshinPlace (TGP)

The Genshin Place, or TGP, formed during Reddit's r/place. An April Foolâ€™s event that brought together all corners of the internet. More information on what happened during r/place and the formation of this community may be found here.

In the aftermath of r/place, the community that had formed held much potential. This potential was taken and molded into a niche that was not yet found in many other Genshin-related spaces. The Genshin community did many things, however, a community to go and talk about lore specifically had not yet taken off. TGP was meant to fill that niche and allow fellow Genshin players a community to have easy access to Genshin-related lore.

Learning more about the lore of any video game is a tedious task to behold. TGP was meant to allow that knowledge to be more comprehensive and attainable. A convenient way to learn more about one's favorite game.

We at TGP aim to create an environment that allows for an undemanding access point for the average Genshin player to gain the tools needed to take a deep dive into lore and theory-crafting.
